Experience

The British School, Rio de Janeiro

Currently working as a Computer Science teacher at The British School Rio de Janeiro. Has been involved in KS3 and IB classes lessons planning and teaching. Also, has participated in several extra curricular activities as: the robotics club, Itatiaia national park field trip and the staff band.

Colegio Marilia Mattoso

Worked at the Marilia Mattoso School as a STEM teacher. Out of the classroom, organized robotics competitions and submitted works for science fairs. Oriented 8 students at the publication of two works (posters) for the IV Feira de Ciencia e Tecnologia de Niteroi.

Laboratorio Nacional de Computacao Cientifica - LNCC

Worked as a scientific intern at the LNCC in the area of modeling of systems and control. The work in the LNCC had as objective make a simulation of the PageRank algorithm, responsible for ranking the pages in the Google search engine.

Laboratorio de Sistemas Inteligentes e Robotica - SIRLab

Worked at SIRLab as a software developer in C ++ language at the VSSSDK project. The project consisted of developing an autonomous robot soccer system for the IEEE Very Small Size Soccer category. Among the areas that constituted the development of the SIRSoccer system, was in charge of the systems and control research and development.
